About Me

From pixels to strategy — a journey built on deep technical foundations.

I'm a Computer Vision and Deep Learning engineer who has evolved into a technical strategy role, working at the intersection of cutting-edge AI research and real-world product impact. With over six years of hands-on experience across defence, healthcare, automotive, and telecom, I've moved from building systems at the hardware level to shaping the technology roadmaps that define them.

Today, as Technical Strategy Engineer at beyond technologies, I drive R&D initiatives, represent the company at C-level engagements and international trade events, and bridge the gap between engineering teams and executive stakeholders. My work sits at the convergence of deep technical expertise and strategic business thinking.

I'm also a PhD candidate at Middle East Technical University, pursuing research in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, building on my MSc work on transformer-based multi-sensor image fusion (FuseFormer, arXiv 2024).
